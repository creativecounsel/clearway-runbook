# AI Tool Instructions

Guidance for using AI tools (e.g., an internal chatbot, Claude, ChatGPT) alongside this runbook — not as a replacement for it.

## What AI tools are good for here

- Quickly checking a draft against Clearway language for obvious mismatches
- Drafting a first pass of comms language to bring to review (still requires human review before publishing)
- Summarizing relevant sections of Archive resources for a specific question

## What AI tools should not be used for

- Final sign-off on any claim — a human reviewer is still required
- Generating new Clearway entries without legal review of the underlying claim
- Answering questions about live legal/regulatory status (these change; the AI tool's knowledge may not be current)

## If using an internal tool trained on this runbook

- Confirm what version of the runbook it was trained/connected to, and how often that refreshes
- Treat its answers as a draft/starting point, not a final clearance
- Report any confidently wrong answers back to the runbook owner — that's a sign either the tool needs retraining or the underlying doc is unclear
