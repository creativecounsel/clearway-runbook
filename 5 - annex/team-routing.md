# Who Handles What

If you're unsure who to ask, tag the runbook owner first and they'll route you.

| Topic | Covers | Route to |
|---|---|---|
| This runbook & review process | Questions about how the runbook works, or general review guidance | Runbook owner |
| IP & third-party marks | Third-party logos, partner names, co-branding, certifications, screenshots | IP specialist/owner |
| AI-related external claims | AI/ML capability claims, model performance claims, AI-positioning language | AI claims owner |
| Privacy & regulatory | Compliance framing, consumer-harm positioning, regulatory claims | Privacy/regulatory owner |
| Internal AI tool usage | Tools used internally for drafting/review support, not customer-facing | Internal tools owner |
| Product-specific questions | What a specific product/feature actually does and its current claims boundaries | Relevant product counsel/owner |
| Geography-specific questions (e.g., EU/UK, other regions) | Region-specific legal requirements that differ from the default | Regional counsel/owner |
