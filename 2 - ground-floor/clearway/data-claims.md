# Clearway: Data Claims

Pre-approved language for describing what data the product collects, how it's used, or how it's protected.

> If your language matches an entry below (or is a close paraphrase), no legal sign-off is needed. If it doesn't match, or you're describing something new or more specific, go to Workshop (`/workshop/legal-reviewing-guidelines.md`) for how to route it.

---

### ✅ Pre-approved phrasing

| Use case | Approved language | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| General security posture | "We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information." | Don't pair with specific certifications unless current with Security. |
| Data usage — general | "We use your information to provide and improve [Product]." | Doesn't extend to other products — that's a broader claim, needs review. |
| No sale of data | "We do not sell your personal information." | Confirm still accurate for the specific product/feature. |
| Encryption | "Your information is encrypted in transit and at rest." | Only if confirmed true for the specific system described. |

### 🚫 Always route to review

- Any claim tied to a specific regulatory framework (GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA)
- Any claim about AI/ML model training on user data
- Any comparative claim ("more secure than X")
- Any claim involving biometric or health data
