# Clearway: Partnership & Co-Marketing Claims

Pre-approved language for describing a partner, integration, or co-marketed relationship.

---

### ✅ Pre-approved phrasing

| Use case | Approved language | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Active integration | "[Product] integrates with [Partner]." | Confirm integration is live, not in beta, before using present tense. |
| General partnership mention | "[Company] has partnered with [Partner] to [general goal]." | Don't specify revenue share, exclusivity, or contract terms. |
| Logo/co-branding usage | Partner logo may be used once trademark guidelines are on file. | Confirm current guidelines — these expire without notice. |

### 🚫 Always route to review

- Any claim implying partner endorsement of your broader product
- Joint press releases or named-partner quotes
- Exclusivity claims
- Any partner without an executed agreement
